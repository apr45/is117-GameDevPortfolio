### Sprint 1: Identity & The "Unity" Principle
**Goal:** Establish the core narrative and build a shared identity with the NJIT/Game Dev audience.
* **Narrative Writing:** Draft a 100–200 word biography focused on the transition from IT major to Game Developer.
* **Shared Language:** Identify 3–5 industry terms (e.g., "Physics Implementation," "State Management") to use as section headers to build peer credibility.
* **Static Wireframing:** Build the basic JSX structure for all scenes to ensure the "one-page" scrollytelling flow works before adding logic.
* **Exit Check:** Confirm the "Story" of the site is clear and professional without any styling or animations.
